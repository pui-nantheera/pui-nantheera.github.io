Projects

Artificial Intelligence in the Creative Industries

AI and data-driven optimisation methods have seen increasing success across many domains. They are also proliferating in the creative sector, influencing technology developments, delivery formats, creative design and production workflows. This project assess opinions, understanding and aspirations across our partner network and beyond, reporting on the opportunities for, and aspirations of, the community, making recommendations for targeted research topics, both within this project and beyond. This will be positioned in the context of global developments in this most rapidly developing field.

Automated Alert System for Volcanic Unrest

Satellite radar (InSAR) can be employed to observe volcanic ground deformation, which has shown a significant statistical link to eruptions. The explosion in data has however brought major challenges associated with timely dissemination of information and distinguishing volcano deformation patterns from noise, which currently relies on manual inspection. Here, we present a novel approach to detect volcanic ground deformation automatically from InSAR images.
